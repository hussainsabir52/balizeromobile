Visit Permit To Enter Multiple Times

A Multiple Entry Visitor Visa is valid for up to 1(one) year from the date of issue and allows subsequent trips to enter Indonesia. It covers non-work purposes that includes, but not limited to:

Transit; holiday/tourism; visiting family; social; official visit; benchmarking; non-commercial sport activities; short courses; comparative studies; short training; business meeting; buying goods or products; giving lectures or attending seminars; participating on international exhibition; attend meeting held by head office or branch office in Indonesia; crew joining a vessel. Multiple entry visa is issued based on prior authorization (immigration clearance) from Directorate General of Immigration applied by the sponsor in Indonesia.
