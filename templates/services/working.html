WORKING PERMIT AS EMPLOYEE

The Work Visa KITAS is issued to foreign nationals who want to work for an Indonesian company or organization.

For a work KITAS, your employer has to obtain a work permit (IMTA) for you from the Indonesian Ministry of Manpower. You will then receive a KITAS based on the duration of your work contract.
