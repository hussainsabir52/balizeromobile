About Us

Make Your Bali Experience Memorable and Safe

Bali Zero was born in 2020 as an international brand from the mother company Bayu Santero (founded locally in 2003) in pandemic time. That's why Zero: with the pandemic Bali had to recreate itself from the scratch.

Bali Zero has legally supported the creation of new businesses, helping the expat community with visas process, residence and work permits. Bali Zero is son of the pandemic, which unintentionally created an extraordinary community of beautiful people. Now we are facing new challenges but always supporting the new generations of expats on the island of Gods.

Our goal is to provide the best services in terms of professionality and efficiency creating a cool network of people to develop social, cultural and psychological growth serving people’s well-being, locally and globally. Chose your service, join our community.
