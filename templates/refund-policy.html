Our Refund Policy

  • It is possible to provide a refund only if the application process at the immigration office is not started yet.
  • We can refund the amount of 80% of the fee paid if the E-Visa is not already provided by the Indonesian Government.
  • If the Indonesian Government closes the borders we will immediately advise you and stop the application process, if your e-Visa meantime has been already approved and expires we cannot provide a refund.
  • A 10% administration fee applies if you cancel your application and request a refund within 3h from the application form submission.
  • Once your e-Visa has been approved we cannot provide a refund.
  • Your refund will be proccessed within 14 business days from request via email directly to our accounts department at : payment@balizero.com.

Always take a look to our Terms & Conditions.
